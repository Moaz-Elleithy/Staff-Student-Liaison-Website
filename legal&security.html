Legal Compliance Statement

Legal compliance is our first priority at Staff-Student Liaison Committee (SSLCom) in order to give our users a safe and reliable online experience. In order to resolve important legal challenges, we have taken the following actions:

Copyright Compliance

The content, graphics, and photos on our website all abide with copyright regulations. We either have the content's ownership rights or the required authorizations or licenses to use it.

Image Sources

Our photos come from reliable sources that offer licensed or royalty-free photos. Where necessary, proper attributions are given in compliance with the agreements under which these platforms are used.

GDPR Compliance

The Staff-Student Liaison Committee (SSLCom) is dedicated to safeguarding user data and privacy. We have taken steps to ensure that we abide by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We describe in our privacy policy how we gather, use, and safeguard user information.

Encryption and Security

Our contact forms encrypt information while it is in transit and at rest. We use advanced encryption techniques to safeguard customer data privacy.

We adhere to the principle of data minimization, keeping contact form information just as long as is necessary to accomplish the stated goal. The information is safely removed from our systems at the end of this time.
