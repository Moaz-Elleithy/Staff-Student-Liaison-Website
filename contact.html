How & Why You Should Use The Form

How:

  • Please fill out the first text box with your email address. This enables us to get in contact with you in case that we require updates or clarification on your comments.
  • Please specify to whom you are sending comments to in the following form. You have the option of selecting a certain year group or committee member.
  • This enables us to make sure that the right people receive your comments and are able to properly handle your concerns.
  • Enter comments in the text entry form. Give us as much information as you can to better understand your point of view.
  • After completing the necessary fields, press the "Submit" button. The Staff-Student Liaison Committee will get your feedback immediately for review.

Why:

  • We appreciate your opinions and think they are essential to improving our community. We have developed an easy-to-use form for you to provide feedback to the Staff-Student Liaison Committee in order to speed up the process. We would love to hear from you, whether you have suggestions, concerns, or positive feedback.
