Welcome to SSLCom!

SSLCom serves as a bridge for communication between faculty, staff, and students regarding topics like instruction, assessments, departmental guidelines, and student concerns. Student representatives can voice their opinions to the department about:

  • student opinions regarding the quality of teaching;
  • contributions to the design of curricular modifications;
  • departmental services (such as personal tutor availability, office hours, study spaces, hand-in arrangements, the departmental network etc.);
  • strengthening the lines of communication between faculty and students.

You can contact one of your student representatives if you would want to share any opinions you may have regarding your course, the university, or anything related to student life at an SSLCom meeting.
